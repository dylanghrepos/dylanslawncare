Tips & Techniques

Seasonal Lawn Care Tips

Spring

Prepare your mower. Change oil, air filter and spark plug. Clean up branches, and twigs. Put mower on a high setting. Reapply mulch.

Summer

During the summer, try to adjust mower for higher grass. Taller grass shades soil. Try not to park on the grass, it can mess up your soil! Also make sure to sharpen your mower blade, dull blades will tear grass.

Winter

During the winter, try to keep your lawn clean. It could suffocate before winter. If your lawn is frosted, try not to walk on it too much. The grass can become weak if walked over too much.

Fall

During the fall, make sure to keep mowing. The grass wont stop growing until it frosts over the winter. Make sure to rake often, it can prevent sunlight to plants and stop them from making food.
